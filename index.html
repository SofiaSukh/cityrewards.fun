About CityRewards.fun

Building the future of civic engagement and sustainable communities

Our Mission

To create a platform where every citizen action contributes to building a more sustainable, connected, and economically vibrant community. We believe that small actions, when multiplied by thousands, can create massive positive change.

Our Vision

A world where cities are regenerative ecosystems where citizens actively participate in their community's growth, sustainability, and prosperity through simple, rewarding actions that benefit everyone.
